State of Orissa v. Bidyabhushan (1963): Tribunal/court cannot direct government to reconsider penalty if dismissal is justified by rules and supported by evidence.
Union of India v. T.R. Varma (1958): Natural justice requires fair hearing and cross-examination.
Daryao v. State of UP (1961): Dismissal of writ petition in limine does not bar subsequent petition.
Tilokchand Motichand v. H.B. Munshi (1970): Res judicata applies to writs only if earlier petition was decided on merits.
Amar Singh v. Custodian Evacuee Property (1957): Rights of quasi-permanent allottees.
Punjab Tin Supply Co. v. Central Government (1984): Time-bound exemption for new buildings under rent control is valid.
Motor General Traders v. State of Andhra Pradesh (1984): Indefinite exemption for new buildings is unconstitutional.
S.P. Sampath Kumar v. Union of India (1987): Upheld constitutional validity of administrative tribunals.
