Displaced Persons (Compensation and Rehabilitation) Act, 1954:
- Section 10: Rights of displaced persons to property allotted on quasi-permanent basis.
- Section 12: Acquisition of evacuee property by Central Government.
- Section 33: Power of Central Government to revise orders.
- Section 36: Compensation for acquired property.
Key case: Amar Singh v. Custodian Evacuee Property (1957): Rights of quasi-permanent allottees.
